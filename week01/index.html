Kent, the most south-eastern part of the United Kingdom, is also known as the "Garden of England" due to its greenery. England is most known for its staple meal of fish and chips, the red phone boxes, being a footballing nation, and of course the Royal Family.

Oslo, Norway

Official Flag of Norway

Norway is known for vikings, tall people, the northern lights, midnight sun in the summer and 24 hours of darkness in the winter. Norway also has a royal family but they love integrating with the people so it is normal to randomly see the Crown Princess on a public bus, or the Queen at the mall. A well-known motto of the Norwegians is that 'there is no bad weather, just bad clothes.', which is hard to believe when temperatures are below 0 Celcius and ice can be present for a good 5-6 months during a bad winter.
