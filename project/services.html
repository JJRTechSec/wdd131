Our Services

One-To-One Classes

With one-to-one classes, you receive undevided attention from the teacher as you are the sole student in the class. This means that if you have questions, concerns or extra help with a particular conept, the teacher is able to address this more directly and personally than if there were more students within the class!

Facebook

Over on our Facebook page, we share daily posts to help you with grammar, vocabulary, common mistakes that learners make and how to correct them, and much more!

Instagram

Over on our Instagram page, we regularly share helpful tips regarding grammar, different uses/meanings of particular words, facts about both Norway and Norwegian, and reels to help you out with the sounds of the language.

YouTube

Our YouTube channel is the place to be for our in-depth tips and guidance as you progress along your Norwegian journey. We look at grammatical explanations, the sounds of the language and many other aspects of the language and country, so you can gain a deeper understanding and appreciation of this beautiful country!
